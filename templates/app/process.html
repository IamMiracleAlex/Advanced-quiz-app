APPLICATION

If you are already a registered candidate, please log in using previously created login information. Forgot your login information? Recover your login data by clicking on the link available for this purpose under the login fields.


PERSONAL INFORMATION
Enter your personal information (name, address, phone number, email, etc.).

EDUCATION
Add your education information. You will be asked to enter your school, major, and education level.

SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS
Questions that ask about your skills, qualifications and certifications.

ASSESSMENT

EduBridge Academy uses online assessments to measure skills and abilities that generally do not emerge from interviews. These assessments are critical, as they help determine your potential for success as a Career Acceleration Program Participant.



ADDITIONAL IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
  • Our Assessments are delivered online which allows 24/7 access and are fully mobily enabled.
  • The assessments require sustained concentration and reading to complete.
  • These assessments may be challenging, so please be sure to take them seriously and complete them in a space void of distractions.
  • The assessment is timed. So, please make sure you take your time and complete any assessment provided to you in an atmosphere that allows you to be comfortable and concentrate fully.

PAYMENT

Successful candidates who passess the assesment stage and gets selected will be required to make payments to continue with the final application process. The Career Acceleration Program costs NGN 50,000.

GET SELECTED

Congratulations! You've proven you have a lot to offer. We look forward to welcoming you to the Career Acceleration Program, where you will work, learn and grow alongside highly talented people, face new and exciting challenges and take on industry designed projects.
